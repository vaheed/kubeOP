# Support

kubeOP is maintained by the community. Choose the channel that best matches your request.

## Bug reports & feature requests

- Open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/vaheed/kubeOP/issues) and include:
  - kubeOP version (`/v1/version` output)
  - Deployment method (Docker Compose, Kubernetes, bare metal)
  - Kubernetes distribution/version and cloud provider (if any)
  - Steps to reproduce, expected behaviour, and actual behaviour

## Security reports

- Email <security@kubeOP.io> with a description of the vulnerability, affected versions, and any available proof of concept.
- Expect acknowledgement within 2 business days and coordinated disclosure once a fix is available.
- Do not create public issues for security vulnerabilities.

## Operational questions

- Use GitHub Discussions (if enabled) or tag your issue with `question` so maintainers can triage appropriately.
- Review [`docs/OPERATIONS.md`](docs/OPERATIONS.md) and [`docs/TROUBLESHOOTING.md`](docs/TROUBLESHOOTING.md) for common scenarios.

## Commercial support

The project does not currently offer paid support. Organisations interested in long-term assistance or training can open a
discussion to explore collaboration options with maintainers.
